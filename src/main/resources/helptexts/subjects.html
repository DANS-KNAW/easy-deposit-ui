Subject' field is used to describe the contents of the research project and the files deposited. This field can be used to fill in all kinds of descriptive keywords. In addition to general characteristics, keywords can also be used to refer to specific finds or results. If the keywords originate from a controlled list of keywords, fill in the name of the thesaurus. If this list of keywords cannot be chosen as a scheme, fill in the keyword followed by the name of the thesaurus in brackets.

NB: Any details on the date are recorded separately at â€˜Temporal coverage' below and need not be included in the general keywords.

Click the button behind a field to add an additional field. It is also possible to fill in several keywords behind each other in one field if they are separated from each other by a semicolon followed by a space (; ).

Unique combination of figures and letters in order to make it possible to identify the research project or the files.

There are currently still multiple identifiers for the same research project. This may vary from an internal project number within the executive organization to the international ISBN number for publications.

After depositing, EASY automatically assigns the Persistent Identifier to the dataset

Click the button behind a field to add an additional field.

Examples

  • 90-77287-02-6 (ISBN)
  • 1836 (Archis Onderzoeksnummer)
  • 52189 (Archis Waarnemingsnummer)
  • NHDA: R0214
  • 0925-6229 (RAAP projectnummer)
  • STAR: P1233

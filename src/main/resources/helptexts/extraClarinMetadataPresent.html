If you have received CLARIN funding for the data that you are about to deposit, your dataset ought to contain (preferably) one or (if necessary) more metadata files in the so-called Component MetaData Infrastructure (CDMI) format. Also, when you have received a research grant that requires you to deliver your results in a CLARIN-compatible way, CMDI metadata should be part of the dataset. For information about CMDI please see: http://www.clarin.eu/cmdi. To enable us to make your CMDI metadata available to e.g. external search engines please select the second option.

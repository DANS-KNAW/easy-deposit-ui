Date created

Date on which the research project was finished and the dataset was completed. This is often a different date from the date on which you deposit the data. The deposit date is added to the metadata automatically. Please note: for archaeological reports use the date of publication

This is the field where you fill in the date on which the research project was finished and, as a result of this, the dataset was completed. Please use the standardized date notation (ISO), in the form of YYYY-[MM]-[DD]. The ‘Date created' will form a part of the source references. If the data have been collected and processed during a medium to long period of time, please fill in the end date here.

NB: At ‘Temporal coverage' on the next tab, you can indicate to what period (date) the research relates. This is where you can indicate, for example, that an early Bronze Age site has been researched.

Click the button behind a field to add an additional field.

Examples

  • 2006
  • 2003-04
  • 2005-03-01

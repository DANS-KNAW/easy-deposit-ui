If the dataset is known under multiple titles, a translation of the title, or has a subtitle, you can enter these titles in this field

When performing a search in EASY, you will be able to find the words from the ‘Title' as well as words from the ‘Alternative title'. However, the ‘Alternative title' is not shown in the list of search results.

Click the button behind a field to add an additional field.

Examples

  • Meuse Valley Project - macro regional investigations
  • Archeologische Rapporten 2008-46

The title of the research.

Full title with an adequate description of the dataset, preferably with geographical and periodical indications. For this purpose, choose the title that is used most, the Dutch or the English title. The title of the dataset will become part of the source references. The best thing to do of course is to stick as closely as possible to the name under which the research project is known in literature.

Examples

  • The Opium Society: Dividend transfers to shareholders in Patria, 1756-1794
  • De mesolithische en vroeg-neolithische vindplaats Hoge Vaart-A27 (Flevoland)
  • Blerick-centrumplan, een bureauonderzoek naar de archaeologische verwachting voor het centrum van Blerick
  • Culturele Veranderingen in Nederland, 1989
  • Health behaviour in school-aged children - HBSC Nederland

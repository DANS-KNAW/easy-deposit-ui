Other analogous or digital resources related to the dataset of this research project.

Use this field to fill in the titles of publications, reports (internal or otherwise), websites and/or other digital resources (datasets, online publications) that belong to the research project. Add a full title description and, where possible, also fill in the web address (URL) where to find this information. By means of a ‘qualifier' it is possible to specify the relation between the dataset deposited here and the other sources referred to. Several examples of qualifiers that often occur:

  • for a literature reference: isReferencedBy;
  • is part of a larger project: isPartOf;
  • replaces a previous deposit: replaces;
  • is replaced by: isReplacedBy.

NB: If you are filling in the address of a website in the URL field, take account of the fact that the website may no longer be accessible after some time. It is recommended to use only ‘persistent identifiers', URLs or identifiers of digital archives. Use the ‘Verify' button to check whether the URL has been written correctly and actually works.

The field ‘Source' can be used to fill in analogous and digital resources that have served as bases for the dataset.

Click the button behind a field to add an additional field.

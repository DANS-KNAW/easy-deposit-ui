You can add the geographical coordinates of the area or site in longitude/latitude degrees or in the Dutch national coordinate system RD (Rijksdriehoekstelsel), as a central point of 2 coordinates. The used reference system needs to be specified in the drop-down menu.

Longitude/latitude coordinates should be entered in decimal degrees. The latitude value should be entered in the ‘X’-box; the longitude in the ‘Y’-box. RD coordinates should be entered in meters as single values, without the use of grouping characters or separators such as dots or commas.

Examples

Coordinate system X Y
Latitude/longitude (degrees) 4.345776 52.080918
RD (in m.) 105350 460350

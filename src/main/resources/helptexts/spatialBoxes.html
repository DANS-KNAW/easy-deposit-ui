You can add the geographical coordinates of the area or site in longitude/latitude degrees or in the Dutch national coordinate system RD (Rijksdriehoekstelsel), as a rectangular box covering the entire research area (4 coordinates: outer East, West, North and South coordinates). The used reference system needs to be specified in the drop-down menu.

Longitude/latitude coordinates should be entered in decimal degrees. The latitude value should be entered in the ‘X’-box; the longitude in the ‘Y’-box. RD coordinates should be entered in meters as single values, without the use of grouping characters or separators such as dots or commas.

Examples

Coordinate system North East South West
RD (in m.) 461355 106555 460150 105350

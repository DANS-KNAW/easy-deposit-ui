Coordinates of the geographical location of the research area or site.

You can add the geographical coordinates of the area in ‘lengte/breedte (graden)’ (longitude/latitude) or in the Dutch national coordinate system RD (Rijksdriehoekstelsel), both as a central point (Point: 2 coordinates) and as a rectangular box covering the entire research area (Box: 4 coordinates). The used reference system needs to be specified in the drop-down menu

Longitude/latitude coordinates should be entered in decimal degrees. The latitude value should be entered in the ‘X’-box; the longitude in the ‘Y’-box. RD coordinates should be entered in meters as single values, without the use of grouping characters or separators such as dots or commas.

Click the button behind a field to add an additional field.

Examples

  • Lengte/breedte (graden) X 4.345776 Y 52.080918
  • RD (in m.) X 105350 Y 460350
  • RD (in m.)
    North 461355
    West 105350 East 106555
    South 460150

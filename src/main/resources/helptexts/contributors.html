First, enter information about the person(s) and/or organisation(s) responsible for the creation of the dataset (mandatory). The dataset will be registered in the name of the "Creator" and this name will subsequently be used in the source references for this dataset. Typically, the principal researcher or the project manager is the (first) creator. You can add more creators.

Optionally, you can select another role from the list for persons and/or organisations that have contributed to the realisation of the dataset in other ways.

Please mention your Digital Author ID (DAI), your International Standard Name Identifier (ISNI) and/or your ORCID. You can find your DAI, ISNI and/or ORCID in NARCIS by selecting the entry PERSONEN (English: PEOPLE) and typing your surname in the SEARCH field.
